Topic

As future designers, the relevance of AI in image generation is increasingly significant and is expected to grow further. The core challenge lies in harnessing the opportunities AI offers: how can designers effectively control and utilize AI to enhance their creative ideas? Developing a clear vision and accurately conveying it to the AI are essential for optimizing outcomes. In an era where artificial intelligence is profoundly altering the creative landscape, engaging with AI image generation has become crucial for future designers.

Advantages of AI for the Design industry

AI tools, such as image generators, are not only innovative but are also transforming the conceptualization and creation of visual content. Mastery of these technologies can unlock new creative possibilities, streamline workflows, and maintain competitiveness in a rapidly evolving industry. AI-generated images can alleviate workload and boost efficiency, enabling rapid prototyping and visualization of complex concepts. Therefore, acquiring skills to provide precise prompts for AI image generation is vital for future professionals. The final project of this semester focused on enhancing understanding of AI’s role in image generation, with the goal of crafting a cohesive visual narrative. The project emphasized the limitless creative possibilities available through AI, allowing for the development of a unified and visually coherent design.

Idea

The project was designed as a group endeavor. Initially, ideas were gathered, and a rough storyline was developed, centered around experiences from the course trip. Keywords derived from these experiences were then fed into the AI (ChatGPT). The project thus became a continuous collaboration between the AI and the team. The narrative generated by the AI was subsequently revised and refined. Ideas for the visual style of the images were collected, and reference images were selected. Establishing a consistent style and color scheme (black/white) was essential for ensuring the successful generation of cohesive images.

Chat GPT helps with generating the storyline

Process

Once the storyline was finalized, the focus shifted to image generation. Selecting an appropriate AI program was crucial. Initially, OpenAIs Dalle 3 was considered based on Fabian's recommendation, but due to various constraints, Adobe Firefly was chosen instead. While Adobe Firefly generally performed well, maintaining a consistent design proved challenging. This issue was partially mitigated by using sample images in the “style” or “texture” sections. Generating characters with consistent features, such as hair and facial attributes, was problematic, as these elements varied significantly despite uniform prompts and references. Consequently, it was decided to depict characters only through partial views, such as the back, hair, or legs. Adobe Firefly’s capabilities, including prompt input and options for style, texture, color, light, and perspective, were valuable for achieving a largely consistent design. Establishing reference images and a predefined color and light scheme beforehand facilitated a more uniform appearance. Each text section was assigned a corresponding image concept, which was then divided among the team members. Flexibility was required as some ideas proved more difficult to realize, particularly complex features like fingers and faces. An exciting aspect of the process was the ability to further edit the generated images and refine specific areas. The images were collected in a group chat, organized, and occasionally edited. A larger volume of image material was generated than needed for the storyline, a notable advantage of using AI. Consequently, a selection of the best images was made. For the final presentation of the project, the images and text elements were incorporated into a website using Flexbox. Flexbox, a CSS layout module, was employed to create a dynamic and visually engaging layout. It simplifies the design and arrangement of website content by providing flexible options for positioning, aligning, and arranging elements within a container.

Adobe Firefly provides various tools for prompt engineering

Takeaways

Overall, the project was successful, despite some arbitrariness in the storyline. The style of the generated images appears to be largely consistent, though there is room for improvement, particularly in the depiction of characters. Exploring the use of color, as opposed to sticking solely with black and white, would be an intriguing development. The opportunity to work with generative AI was highly valued. Given the opaque nature of AI, the “learning by doing” approach remains essential. Proficiency with such tools and ongoing engagement with them will be crucial for future designers. The project highlighted both the advantages and limitations of AI programs. AI can rapidly and efficiently visualize ideas, significantly reducing the time required compared to traditional methods. However, the core challenge remains in the creative thought and its articulation in language. The limitations of current AI technology were also evident. Certain ideas could only be partially realized, with notable difficulties in rendering faces and hands. The quality of AI-generated results has yet to match that of traditional drawings and sketches, though improvements may occur in the future. The project underscored the importance of patience in working with AI, as well as the need to manage resources effectively and refine the communication of creative ideas.
